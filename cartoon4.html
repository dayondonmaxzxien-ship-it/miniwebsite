Super Duck

A duck discovered it could fly faster than any other bird in the forest. It loved testing its speed and helping friends in need. The other animals watched in amazement at its flying skills. The duck felt proud and confident.

One day, a fire broke out in the forest, endangering many creatures. Super Duck quickly helped by guiding them to safety. It fetched water from the pond to help control the flames. The animals praised its bravery and quick thinking.

The duck became a forest hero, admired by all animals. Its courage inspired others to act bravely too. Super Duck proved that talents can save lives. Everyone learned that helping others is a true act of heroism.
