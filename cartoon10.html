The Happy Cloud

A cheerful cloud floated across the sky, forming playful shapes for children below. People loved watching the cloud create animals, castles, and funny faces. The cloud enjoyed making everyone smile. Its fluffy appearance was magical and delightful.

Children ran to see what new shapes the cloud would make each day. Adults smiled, enjoying the simple pleasures. The cloud’s movements inspired imagination and creativity. Everyone looked forward to its daily performances.

The happy cloud taught that joy can come from small things. Even a simple cloud can brighten someone’s day. People learned to appreciate beauty in everyday life. The cloud reminded everyone to find happiness in the ordinary.
